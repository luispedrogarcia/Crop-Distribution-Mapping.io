Food Distribution within groundwater basins

Hello, my name is Luis Garcia and I'm a Geography major emphasizing in GIS with a huge interest in anything to do with Agriculture, farmers' markets, and food waste. This project entails looking at agricultural crops differently in California, by looking at it from a ground water perspective. The point of the project is to click on any sub ground water basin feature and you'll get the distribution of crops. However before anything, it should NOT be assumed that the crops located within these sub ground water basins are using that exact same water they are located in, as the crops might use water that may be located from different regions.
